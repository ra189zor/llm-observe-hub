Powerful Features

Everything you need to monitor and optimize your local LLM performance

Real-time Analytics

Monitor latency, throughput, and performance metrics in real-time. Get instant insights into your model's behavior with comprehensive dashboards.

Model Comparison

Compare performance across different models with A/B testing, side-by-side metrics, and AI-powered recommendations for optimal model selection.

Cost Tracking

Track token usage costs and electricity consumption. Set budgets, get alerts, and optimize your spending with detailed cost breakdowns.

Smart Alerts

Configure intelligent alerts for performance issues, cost overruns, and anomalies. Stay informed with real-time notifications.

Streaming Support

Full support for streaming LLM requests with specialized metrics like Time to First Token (TTFT) and streaming throughput analysis.

AI Optimization

Get AI-powered suggestions for performance improvements, parameter tuning, and hardware optimization based on your usage patterns.
